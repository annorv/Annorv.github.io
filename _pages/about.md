I am a PhD Researcher in the Built Environment at the University of 
Wolverhampton, where my research sits at the intersection of game theory, 
artificial intelligence, and serious games to drive decarbonisation in the 
UK health sector, with a particular focus on the NHS.

My work explores how game-based methodologies and AI can be used to engage 
stakeholders, model complex energy decision-making, and accelerate net-zero 
transitions in healthcare buildings and infrastructure.

Alongside my PhD, I work as a Principal Energy Consultant with a background 
in data science and mathematics, bringing over a decade of industry experience 
to my academic research.

## Research Interests
- Building decarbonisation and net-zero strategy
- Serious games and gamification for stakeholder engagement
- Game theory and AI in energy decision-making
- Healthcare estates and NHS sustainability
